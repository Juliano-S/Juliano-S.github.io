FAQ

Frequently asked questions:

Q:How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?
A:You can print a document from your laptop at HZ by connecting to the printer named "HZ printer" by going to hz.mynetpay.nl, via that website you can print the document you need to print and pay for your print.

Q:How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?
A: To scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ, You should open up Windows Fax and Scan (or MacOS Image Capture if you are using a Mac). Then place your document face down onto the scanner bed of the HZ printer and select “Scan.” Your scanned image will appear in either program automatically; from there, save it onto your laptop as an image file or PDF.1

Q:How can I buy something on the HZ web shop?
A:To buy something on the HZ webshop, you must first log into the webshop with your HZ account. After you have logged in you can add an item to your liking to your shopping cart by clicking "Bestel". You can now go to your shopping cart and order the item by clicking on "afrekenen" after which you will be given payment options. After payment has been completed, your item will have been succesfully ordered.

Q:How can you book a project space in one of the wings?
A:You can book a project space in one of the wings of the HZ by heading over to your personal portal.hz.nl page. Then you must search your personal menu for the page named "Selfservice Portal". This will take you to a website where you can make a reservation. Reservations can be made by clicking on the square with the text "Maak een reservering". This will take you to a page where you can select what you want to reserve, here you must select the option "Reserveer een ruimte", here you can view which rooms are available and then reserve a room.

Q:What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?
A:There is parking space available at Poelendaelesingel 10 in Middelburg. To enter the parking lot, you must first have your HZ card with you and use it to be admitted to the parking lot. After passing the gate you must follow the signs pointing towards HZ parking spaces. HZ parking spaces can be identified by white signs decorated with the HZ logo. If you want to charge your electric car, there are a few spots that allow electric charging. There is a 300 meter walk between the school and the parking lot.
