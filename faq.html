Q:How can you book a project space in one of the wings?
A:You can book a project space in one of the wings of the HZ by heading over to your personal portal.hz.nl page.
Then you must search your personal menu for the page named "Selfservice Portal".
This will take you to a website where you can make a reservation.
Reservations can be made by clicking on the square with the text "Maak een reservering".
This will take you to a page where can select what you want to reserve,
here you must select the option "Reserveer een ruimte",
here you can view which rooms are available and then reserve a room.

Q:What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?
A:There is parking space available at Poelendaelesingel 10 in Middelburg.
To enter the parking lot, you must first have your HZ card with you and use it to be admitted to the parking lot.
After passing the gate you must follow the signs pointing towards HZ parking spaces.
HZ parking spaces can be identified by white signs decorated with the HZ logo. If you want to charge your electric car,
there are a few spots that allow electric charging. There is a 300 meter walk between the school and the parking lot.
